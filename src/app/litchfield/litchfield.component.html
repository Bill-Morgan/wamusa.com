A friendly community located in Central Illinois, Litchfield has the best of everything. From the busy downtown business district o the beautiful recreational areas, Litchfield has it all!

St. Francis Hospital, many small factories, farms and abundant retail stores provide employment opportunities to the residents. Litchfield benefits from a wide range of affordable housing, from newly built subdivisions to the stately older homes.

Lake Lou Yaeger is a popular attraction for visitors from all over the country. This 1500 acre lake features boating, swimming, fishing, camping and much more.

Litchfield community is equally proud of its schools. With the recent addition of Lincoln Land Community College to the town, educational opportunities are available to everyone.


Ask anyone in town, the most valuable
resource in Litchfield is its people!
